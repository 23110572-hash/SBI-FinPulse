# RBI Customer Protection (Summary)

## Limited Liability on Unauthorised Transactions
- **Zero liability** for the customer if the fraud is due to bank negligence, or if reported within 3 working days of unauthorised electronic transaction.
- Limited liability (capped) if reported within 4–7 working days.
- Customers must be able to report fraud 24x7 via SMS/email/phone.

## Grievance Redressal
- Banks must have an internal grievance redressal mechanism with defined turnaround times.
- Unresolved complaints can be escalated to the **RBI Integrated Ombudsman Scheme**.

## Transparency
- Charges, penalties and interest must be communicated clearly.
- Changes in terms require prior notice.

## Fair Treatment
- No coercive recovery practices.
- Recovery agents must follow a code of conduct (no harassment, calls only within permitted hours).
