# RBI Digital Lending Guidelines 2022 (Summary)

Key requirements for digital lending:

- **Direct disbursal**: Loan amounts must be credited directly to the borrower's bank account — no routing through third parties / lending service providers.
- **Direct repayment**: Repayments flow directly from the borrower to the regulated entity.
- **Key Fact Statement (KFS)**: A standardised KFS disclosing the all-inclusive Annual Percentage Rate (APR), fees, and recovery mechanism must be given before the loan.
- **Cooling-off / look-up period**: Borrowers can exit the loan within a cooling-off period by paying principal + proportionate APR without penalty.
- **No automatic credit limit increase**: Credit limits cannot be increased without the borrower's explicit consent.
- **Data privacy**: Apps may collect only data that is need-based, with prior explicit consent; no access to phone contacts, media or location without justification.
- **Grievance redressal**: A nodal grievance officer must be designated and disclosed.
- **Transparency on charges**: No hidden fees; all costs disclosed in the KFS.
