# RBI KYC Norms (Summary)

Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements under RBI Master Direction:

- **Customer identification**: Verify identity and address using Officially Valid Documents (OVDs) — Aadhaar, PAN, passport, voter ID, driving licence.
- **Video-based Customer Identification Process (V-CIP)**: Banks may onboard customers digitally via secure video KYC.
- **Risk categorisation**: Customers classified as low/medium/high risk; due diligence scales with risk.
- **Periodic updation**: Re-KYC required periodically (low-risk every 10 years, high-risk every 2 years).
- **PAN/Form 60**: Mandatory for account opening and high-value transactions.
- **Beneficial ownership**: For non-individual accounts, identify the beneficial owners.
- **No account without KYC**: Accounts cannot be opened or operated without valid KYC.
