# SBI Credit Cards

Use credit responsibly. Interest applies on revolving balances. Review the Key Fact Statement and Most Important Terms & Conditions (MITC).

## SBI SimplyCLICK Credit Card
- Best for: online shopping.
- 10X reward points on partner online merchants (Amazon, etc.), 5X on other online spends.
- Annual fee: ₹499 (reversed on spends ≥ ₹1 lakh/year).
- Welcome Amazon voucher.

## SBI SimplySAVE Credit Card
- Best for: everyday/offline spending.
- 10X reward points on dining, groceries, movies, departmental stores.
- Annual fee: ₹499 (reversible on spends).

## SBI BPCL Credit Card
- Best for: fuel.
- 4.25% value back on fuel at BPCL outlets, plus reward points.
- Annual fee: ₹499.

## SBI Card ELITE
- Premium lifestyle card.
- Complimentary movie tickets, airport lounge access (domestic + international), milestone benefits.
- Annual fee: ₹4,999.

## SBI Card PRIME
- Mid-premium card with accelerated rewards, lounge access, milestone vouchers.
- Annual fee: ₹2,999.

## Reward Points
- Typically 1 reward point ≈ ₹0.25; redeemable for vouchers/statement credit.

## Eligibility
- Salaried or self-employed with stable income.
- Good credit score (CIBIL ~750+) improves approval and limit.
- KYC + income proof required.

## How to Apply
- Via YONO app, sbicard.com, or branch. Pre-approved offers may be available for existing customers.
