# YONO by SBI

YONO = **You Only Need One**. SBI's integrated digital banking and lifestyle app.

## Core Features
- **Account opening**: open a savings account digitally with Aadhaar-based video KYC.
- **FD/RD booking**: open and manage fixed and recurring deposits instantly.
- **Loans**: pre-approved personal loans, home loan, car loan applications.
- **Insurance**: buy SBI Life and SBI General insurance policies.
- **Investments**: invest in SBI Mutual Fund SIPs and lump sums.
- **Bill payments & recharges**: utilities, mobile, DTH via BBPS.
- **UPI**: send/receive money, scan & pay.
- **YONO Cash**: cardless ATM withdrawal using the app + a one-time code.
- **YONO Marketplace**: shopping, travel and lifestyle offers from partner brands.

## YONO Lite vs YONO (full)
- **YONO Lite**: lightweight app focused on core banking (fund transfers, bill pay, account services).
- **YONO (full)**: complete suite — banking + investments + insurance + marketplace + lifestyle.

## Registration
- Existing customers: register using ATM card / internet banking credentials.
- New customers: open an account directly within the app.

## Security Features
- MPIN / biometric (fingerprint, face) login.
- Two-factor authentication and OTP for transactions.
- Device binding and session encryption.
