# SBI Insurance Products

Insurance is the subject matter of solicitation. A free-look period of 15 days applies to life and health policies. T&C apply.

## SBI Life eShield Next (Term Insurance)
- Pure protection term plan — high cover at low cost.
- Cover options from ₹50 lakh to several crore; commonly ₹1 crore.
- Indicative premium: a healthy 30-year-old non-smoker can get **₹1 crore cover from ~₹700–800/month**.
- Multiple options: Level Cover, Increasing Cover, Level Cover with Future-Proofing.
- Tax benefit: premiums deductible under Section 80C; death benefit tax-free under 10(10D).
- Best for: anyone with dependents or liabilities (home loan etc.).

## SBI Life Smart Shield
- Term plan with flexible cover options and rider add-ons (accidental death, disability).

## SBI Life Smart Champ Insurance
- Child plan: guaranteed payouts at key education milestones; secures child's future even if parent is not around.

## SBI Life Retire Smart (Pension/ULIP)
- Unit-linked pension plan to build a retirement corpus.
- Provides regular income (annuity) post-retirement.

## SBI General Health Insurance
- **Super Health Insurance / Arogya** plans: individual and family floater.
- Sum insured options from ₹1 lakh up to ₹50 lakh+.
- Cashless treatment at network hospitals, pre/post hospitalisation cover.
- Indicative premium: family floater ₹10 lakh from ~₹900–1,200/month (varies by age/city).
- Tax benefit: premiums deductible under **Section 80D** (up to ₹25,000; ₹50,000 for senior citizens).

## Why Insurance Matters
- Term insurance protects your family's finances if you are not around.
- Health insurance protects savings from large medical bills.
- Buy term insurance early — premiums are lower at younger ages.

## Claim Process
- Intimate the insurer, submit claim form + documents (death certificate / hospital bills).
- Cashless health claims processed via TPA at network hospitals.
- 15-day free-look period to return the policy if unsatisfied.
