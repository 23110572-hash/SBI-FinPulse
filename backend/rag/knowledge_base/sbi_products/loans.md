# SBI Loans

Loan approval is subject to eligibility, income and credit score. APR, processing fees and a Key Fact Statement are provided as per RBI norms.

## SBI Home Loan
- Interest rate: from **~8.50% p.a.** (linked to EBLR/repo rate; varies with CIBIL score and loan amount).
- Maximum tenure: up to **30 years**.
- No prepayment penalty on floating-rate home loans.
- Processing fee: low (often concessional/nil during offers).
- Eligibility: salaried and self-employed; based on income and repayment capacity.

## SBI Personal Loan (Xpress Credit)
- Interest rate: from **~10.5% p.a.** onward.
- Loan amount up to ₹20–35 lakh depending on profile.
- Tenure up to 6 years.
- Minimal documentation for salaried customers with salary account.
- Pre-approved offers visible in the YONO app.

## SBI Car Loan
- Interest rate: from **~9.0% p.a.**
- Finance up to 85–90% of on-road price.
- Tenure up to 7 years; no prepayment penalty.

## SBI Education Loan
- For higher studies in India and abroad.
- Concessional rates; **moratorium** = course period + 6–12 months before EMIs start.
- Collateral-free up to ₹7.5 lakh (under CGFSEL scheme).
- Tax benefit on interest under Section 80E.

## SBI Gold Loan (Personal Gold Loan)
- Quick loan against gold ornaments.
- Interest rate: from **~9.0% p.a.**
- Loan-to-value (LTV) up to 75% of gold value (RBI cap).
- Minimal documentation, fast disbursal — good for short-term needs.

## EMI Example
- ₹50 lakh home loan at 8.5% for 20 years ≈ EMI of ~₹43,400/month.
- Use the YONO EMI calculator before applying.

## How to Apply
- Via **YONO app** → Loans, or any SBI branch.
- Documents: KYC, income proof (salary slips/ITR), bank statements, property/vehicle papers as applicable.
