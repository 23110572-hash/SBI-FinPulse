# Government Schemes (available via SBI)

## Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)
- For a girl child below 10 years.
- Interest rate: ~**8.2% p.a.** (revised quarterly by Govt), compounded annually.
- Deposit: ₹250 to ₹1.5 lakh per year.
- Tax: EEE — deduction under 80C, interest and maturity tax-free.
- Matures after 21 years; partial withdrawal for higher education at age 18.

## Public Provident Fund (PPF)
- Interest rate: ~**7.1% p.a.** (revised quarterly), compounded annually.
- Lock-in: 15 years (extendable in 5-year blocks).
- Deposit: ₹500 to ₹1.5 lakh per year.
- Tax: EEE — 80C deduction, tax-free interest and maturity.
- Can be opened at SBI branches or via YONO.

## National Pension System (NPS)
- Market-linked retirement scheme regulated by PFRDA.
- Extra tax deduction of ₹50,000 under **Section 80CCD(1B)** (over and above 80C).
- Choice of equity/corporate/government bond allocation.
- Partial withdrawal allowed; 40% of corpus must buy an annuity at retirement.

## Atal Pension Yojana (APY)
- For unorganised sector workers aged 18–40.
- Guaranteed pension of ₹1,000–₹5,000/month after age 60.
- Government co-contribution for eligible subscribers.

## Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)
- Zero-balance basic savings account.
- Includes RuPay debit card, accident insurance cover, and overdraft facility.

## Senior Citizen Savings Scheme (SCSS)
- For individuals aged 60+ (or 55+ for certain retirees).
- Interest rate: ~**8.2% p.a.**, paid quarterly.
- Maximum investment ₹30 lakh; 5-year tenure (extendable).
- 80C deduction available; ideal for retirees seeking regular income.
