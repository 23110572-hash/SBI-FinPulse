# SBI Fixed Deposits (FD)

Safe, fixed-return deposits. Rates below are indicative for deposits under ₹3 crore (retail). Always confirm the live rate on sbi.co.in.

## Indicative FD Interest Rates (General Public)
| Tenure | Rate (p.a.) |
| --- | --- |
| 7 days to 45 days | ~3.50% |
| 46 days to 179 days | ~5.50% |
| 180 days to 210 days | ~6.25% |
| 211 days to less than 1 year | ~6.50% |
| 1 year to less than 2 years | ~6.80% |
| 2 years to less than 3 years | ~7.00% |
| 3 years to less than 5 years | ~6.75% |
| 5 years to 10 years | ~6.50% |

- **Senior citizens** get an additional **0.50%** over the above rates.
- Minimum deposit: **₹1,000**, no upper limit.

## Tax Saving FD
- 5-year lock-in fixed deposit.
- Eligible for deduction up to **₹1.5 lakh under Section 80C**.
- Interest is taxable; premature withdrawal not allowed during lock-in.

## SBI WeCare Deposit (Senior Citizens)
- Special scheme for resident senior citizens.
- Additional premium of 0.50% (over the 0.50% senior citizen rate) for "5 years and above" tenure.
- Designed to protect senior citizens' interest income.

## SBI Amrit Vrishti / Special Tenure Schemes
- SBI periodically offers special-tenure deposits (e.g., 444 days) at slightly higher rates. Check current offers.

## Key Features
- Premature withdrawal allowed with a penalty (typically 0.50%–1.00% lower interest).
- Auto-renewal option on maturity.
- Loan/overdraft against FD up to ~90% of deposit value.
- TDS applies if interest exceeds ₹40,000/year (₹50,000 for seniors); submit Form 15G/15H to avoid if eligible.

## Best For
- Emergency fund parking, capital preservation, senior citizens seeking steady income.
