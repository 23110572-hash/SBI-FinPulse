# SBI Savings Accounts

State Bank of India offers a range of savings accounts to suit different needs.

## Interest Rate
- SBI savings account interest rate: approximately **2.70% p.a.** for balances below ₹10 crore (rate revised periodically; check sbi.co.in for the current figure).
- Interest is calculated on daily closing balance and credited quarterly.

## SBI Basic Savings Bank Deposit (BSBD) Account
- Zero minimum balance, no maintenance charges.
- Free RuPay ATM-cum-debit card.
- Best for: first-time bank users, students, financial inclusion.
- No requirement to maintain Average Monthly Balance (AMB).

## SBI Savings Account (Regular)
- No minimum balance requirement for regular savings accounts (AMB requirement was removed).
- Free unlimited deposits.
- Net banking, mobile banking and YONO access included.
- Cheque book, passbook and ATM-cum-debit card facilities.

## SBI Salary Account
- Zero balance account for salaried employees.
- Free unlimited ATM transactions across banks.
- Complimentary personal accident insurance cover (varies by package: Silver, Gold, Diamond, Platinum).
- Auto-sweep facility, overdraft up to 2 months' net salary (eligibility based).
- Best for: salaried professionals.

## SBI YONO Savings Account (Insta Plus / Digital Savings)
- Fully digital, paperless account opening via the YONO app using Aadhaar + PAN.
- Video KYC available — no branch visit needed.
- Instant account number, virtual debit card.
- Best for: digitally active young customers.

## Who Should Choose What
- Students / low income: Basic Savings (BSBD).
- Salaried: Salary Account for the insurance + zero balance benefits.
- Digital-first users: YONO Insta Plus.

## How to Open
- Via YONO app (Aadhaar-based video KYC) in minutes, or
- At any SBI branch with KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, photograph).
